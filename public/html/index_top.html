KLRN Passport Referrals

These are referrals to KLRN's Passport donation page. Users who are not signed in as KLRN members are sent to the donation page after clicking a start-watching button. Daily referrals can also include referrals not associated with a video.

NOTE: PBS updates accidentally started dropping referral queries in summer 2024, but the issue was fixed in the first week of January 2024.
